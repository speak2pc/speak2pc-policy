Not working?

🔗

Check the connection. Phone and PC must be linked one way: same Wi-Fi, phone hotspot, or USB tethering. Mobile data alone will not work.

📱

Using hotspot or USB? Turn it on in your phone's Settings → Network → Hotspot & tethering, make sure the PC is connected to it, then scan the QR again.

🖥

Is the PC app open? The Speak2PC window must be running on your computer.

📷

Changed the connection? Switched Wi-Fi, hotspot, or cable? Scan the QR code again. It takes 5 seconds.

🎙

Mic not hearing you? Allow the microphone permission when the app asks.

Your voice never leaves your local connection. Encrypted phone ↔ PC. No account needed.
